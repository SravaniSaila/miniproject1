What are the new GST rates in India?
Earlier there were multiple GST rate slabs like 0%, 5%, 12%, 18% and 28%. Now they’ve been streamlined to 5%(merit rate), 18%(standard rate) and 40%(luxury goods). The new GST rates will be effective from September 22, 2025, with the exception of tobacco products.
When will I be able to buy products with the new GST rates?
TeacherThe new GST rates will apply to all orders placed/shipped on or after September 22, 2025.
Will the upcoming GST rate changes affect the speed of deliveries, replacements, or exchanges?
No, deliveries, replacements, or exchanges will happen as per usual timelines based on your location.
